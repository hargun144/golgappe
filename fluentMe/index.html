Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI analyze my speech?

Our AI model listens for specific phonetic sounds, pitch, and speech patterns. It compares your pronunciation to native speakers and provides targeted, corrective feedback in real-time to help you improve.

Is FluentMe suitable for beginners?

Yes, absolutely! FluentMe is designed for all levels, from beginners who want to build a solid foundation to advanced learners who want to refine their fluency and master a specific accent. The app customizes a plan just for you.

What languages are supported?

Currently, FluentMe offers comprehensive training for English (American and British accents). We are actively working to add more languages, including Spanish, French, and Mandarin, in the near future!
